# Buyer Review Guide

## What Buyers Should Look For

1. Can the system show the proposed movement?
2. Can it show who/what had standing?
3. Can it show authority?
4. Can it show evidence sufficiency?
5. Can it show scope?
6. Can it show custody?
7. Can it refuse?
8. Can it prove no-bind?
9. Can it prevent MACI or workflow from binding independently?
10. Can it generate a receipt?
11. Can it replay the result?
12. Can it replay under changed conditions?

## Buyer Interpretation

This repository is not asking buyers to trust a dashboard. It shows whether a proposed movement was admitted, refused, held, escalated, narrowed, or conditionally admitted before protected consequence could form.

## Failure-Closed Buyer Standard

If standing, authority, evidence, scope, custody, language certainty, or route closure fails, the buyer should expect no automatic binding.
