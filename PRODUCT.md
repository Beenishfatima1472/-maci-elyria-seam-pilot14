# Elyria–MACI Product Brief

## Product category

Operational sandbox harness for domain/context signal packages that require governed execution-boundary evaluation before consequence binds.

## Positioning

MACI is the customer-facing domain/context layer.

Elyria/VERITA resolves consequence-admissibility.

## Core proof

MACI may inform.

MACI may not independently bind consequence.

Every consequence-bearing path must pass through the boundary result.

## Review lanes

- structured language-context review
- structured input-context review
- consequence-signal review
- receipt generation
- replay verification
- no-bind enforcement
